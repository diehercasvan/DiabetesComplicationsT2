1. Definition

Diabetic nephropathy (DN) is a term used to define kidney microangiopathic changes caused by diabetes mellitus.1

Diabetic nephropathy (DN) is a chronic complication of patients with Diabetes Mellitus (DM). In DN, renal microcirculation disturbances occur, leading to kidney structure and function impairment, especially in glomerulus.1
Clinically, DN is characterized by proteinuria, hypertension and renal function impairment.1
