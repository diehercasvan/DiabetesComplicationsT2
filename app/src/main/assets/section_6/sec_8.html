9. Prevention

Primary:4

  • Improve screening coverage
  • Patient and family education
  • Promote self-care and healthy lifestyles
  • Frequent ophthalmological examination
  • Adequate control of blood sugar levels, blood pressure and lipids
  • Education for health personnel

Secondary:4

  • Improve screening coverage
  • Frequent ophthalmological examination
  • Early detection and appropriate treatment of DR
  • Ensure early treatment with laser photocoagulation
  • Adequate control of blood sugar levels, blood pressure and lipids
  • Patient and family education
  • Patient and family education

Tertiary:4

  • Frequent ophthalmological examination
  • Ensure restoration of lost vision
  • Adequate treatment of diabetic macular edema or vitreous hemorrhage
  • Adequate control of blood sugar levels, blood pressure and lipids
  • Patient and family education
  • Education for health personnel
