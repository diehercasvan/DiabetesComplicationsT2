4. Risk factors

The main factors associated with DR are diabetes duration and hyperglycemia severity (poor metabolic control).7After 5 years of type 1 DM onset, 25% of patients develop DR. This percentage increases to 80% after 15 years of DM1 onset.7 Within first 5 years of diabetes onset, between 24% and 40% of patients with DM2 have DR. This percentage increases between 53% and 84% after 19 years of evolution of the disease.8

Other risk factors for DR include: hypertension, dyslipidemia, and undiagnosed diabetes.7,8
